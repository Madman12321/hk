Some reliable sources for medical data include:
1. PubMed: A comprehensive database of biomedical literature.
2. WHO (World Health Organization): Provides global health information and statistics.
3. CDC (Centers for Disease Control and Prevention): Offers data on public health and diseases.
4. ClinicalTrials.gov: A registry of privately and publicly supported clinical studies.
5. HealthData.gov: Provides access to various health-related datasets.
